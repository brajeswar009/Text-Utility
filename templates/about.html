Magic Text

Magic Text is a website designed for the people who wants to save time by automating some boring stuffs like removing unwanted punctuations, making every character capital, removing additional spaces or removing new lines from a paragraph or set of paragraphs, which would take plenty of time if done manually. Our visitors time is most valuable to us.
